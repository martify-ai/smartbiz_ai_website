About Matify AI & LaunchKit

Learn how we're helping Gen Z solopreneurs and small businesses launch smarter, faster, and better with AI.

Our Mission

LaunchKit AI is built to empower creators, students, and emerging entrepreneurs to kickstart their ventures without the usual barriers. By leveraging artificial intelligence and automation, we make launching a brand, building a website, and setting up social/WhatsApp systems as simple as clicking a button.

Why Matify AI?

Matify AI was born out of a need to make tech, branding, and automation tools accessible for everyone—especially the underdogs. We’re building a future where you don’t need a degree, funding, or a tech team to bring your ideas to life.

Who It’s For

Whether you're a Gen Z creative, a local business owner, or just someone with a dream and a drive, LaunchKit AI gives you the essentials—done-for-you brand kits, websites, cheat sheets, and automations—to help you win.
